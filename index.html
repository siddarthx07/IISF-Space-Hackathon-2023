Project Overview

Our award-winning project focused on analyzing and predicting Land Use and Land Cover (LULC) changes in Hyderabad, India, leveraging Google Earth Engine and advanced machine learning algorithms. Using satellite imagery and geospatial datasets, we developed a comprehensive system for tracking urban development and environmental changes from 2017 to 2022.
